### Overview


# Maps & Geodata

	- Map tiles/SDK: TomTom Maps (Web/SDK). Alternatively generic OSM tiles where legally permitted.
  	- Geocoding/Reverse: TomTom Search API.
  	- Places/Autocomplete: TomTom Search API; local fallbacks only if required by regulation.

# Routing, ETA & Traffic

  	- Routing & ETA: TomTom Routing API (traffic enabled).
  	- Map‑matching: TomTom Snap‑to‑Roads or internal history if TomTom unavailable.
  	- Traffic/ETA: TomTom Traffic; augment with historical travel‑time models.

# Push, SMS, Voice & Chat

  	- Push: APNs (iOS), FCM (Android, availability permitting), Huawei Push Kit (HMS devices).
  	- SMS/OTP: Local SMS aggregator or direct SMPP to telcos; international providers (Twilio/Infobip/Vonage) often not usable.
  	- Voice/call masking: Local SIP trunk + Asterisk/FreeSWITCH; international voice APIs often restricted.
  	- In‑app chat: Self‑hosted Matrix/Rocket.Chat; managed (Sendbird/Stream) may be unavailable.

 
# Realtime & Backend

  	- Realtime location/dispatch: WebSockets (Socket.IO), MQTT (EMQX/Mosquitto) — self‑hosted.
  	- Hosting: Own servers/VPS/datacenter; major clouds (AWS/GCP/Azure) frequently restricted.
  	- Data & storage: PostgreSQL + PostGIS, Redis, MinIO (S3‑compatible), Nginx as reverse proxy/CDN.

# Monitoring, Analytics & Quality

  	- Crash reporting: Sentry (self‑hosted) or Crashlytics (availability permitting).
  	- Analytics: Own dashboards/logs (no third‑party platforms).
  	- Observability: Prometheus + Grafana; logs via ELK/OpenSearch.

# Support & Operations

  	- Helpdesk: Zammad/OTRS (self‑hosted). International ones (Zendesk/Freshdesk) may be restricted.
  	- Email/notifications: Own SMTP or local provider. Messenger integrations only if legally/technically possible.
